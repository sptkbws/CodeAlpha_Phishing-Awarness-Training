🔍 Introduction to Phishing

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations or individuals via emails, messages, or websites to steal sensitive data like login credentials, credit card numbers, or personal information.

  • A form of social engineering attack
  • Delivered via email, SMS, websites, or calls
  • Goal: Steal data, install malware, hijack accounts

⚠️ Why Phishing is Dangerous

  • 📉 Financial Loss: Credit card theft, fraud
  • 🔓 Data Breach: Leaks of personal or company data
  • 🧠 Identity Theft: Fake accounts in your name
  • 💻 Malware Infections: Ransomware, spyware
  • 🧑‍💼 Reputation Damage: Loss of trust
  • 🏢 Corporate Espionage: Stealing company secrets

🔄 How Phishing Works

  1. Attacker creates fake email/website
  2. Victim receives a convincing message
  3. Clicks fake link or attachment
  4. Enters credentials or downloads malware
  5. Attacker gains access to data/accounts

📧 How to Spot a Phishing Email

  • ❌ Unusual sender address
  • 📎 Unexpected attachments or links
  • ❗ Urgent or threatening language
  • 🔤 Poor grammar or formatting
  • 🌐 Mismatched or suspicious URLs

🌐 Spotting Fake Websites

  • 🕵️ Check the URL carefully (e.g., go0gle.com)
  • 🔒 Look for HTTPS and padlock icon
  • 🧱 Poor design and broken language
  • 🔗 Popups or forced downloads
  • 🧪 Use VirusTotal or Google Safe Browsing

🎭 Social Engineering Tactics

  • ⏳ Urgency: "Act now!" threats
  • 🎁 Tempting Offers: "You won an iPhone!"
  • 🧍 Impersonation: Fake HR, CEO, bank emails
  • 🧨 Fear: "Legal case filed!"
  • 🤝 Trust Exploitation: Fake password resets

🛡️ Best Practices to Stay Safe

✅ DOs

  • Hover over links before clicking
  • Use 2FA for all accounts
  • Verify sender addresses
  • Keep antivirus updated
  • Report suspicious emails to IT/admin

❌ DON'Ts

  • Don't click unknown links or attachments
  • Don't trust emails asking for OTPs/passwords
  • Don't share sensitive info over email/chat

🌍 Real-World Phishing Examples

  • 🧪 Google Docs Scam (2017): Fake shared doc login
  • 🏦 PayPal Fake Alert: Lookalike PayPal login page
  • 🛑 COVID-19 Scams: Fake government relief forms

🧠 Interactive Quiz: Phish or Legit?

Q1: Email says “Update your banking info to avoid account suspension.”
Q2: Website URL: https://googIe-security.com
Q3: Email from HR with an attachment `salary_details.exe`

📌 Key Takeaways

  • 🎣 Phishing = fake emails/websites to steal info
  • 👀 Always check links & senders
  • 🧠 Social engineering uses urgency & fear
  • 🛡️ Use 2FA and report anything suspicious
  • 🚨 Stay alert = stay safe
